## Flagship Profile Selection Rules

Prioritise laureates who collectively provide:

- Historical breadth
- Field diversity
- Geographic diversity
- Different career paths
- Strong public-source availability
- Major explanatory value
- Connections across awards and fields

Do not select only the most famous names.

## Completeness Transparency

Each award page must state:

- Number of records included
- Earliest and latest year covered
- Missing known years
- Last verification date
- Completeness status
