# Security, Privacy, Copyright, and Legal Rules

## Secrets

- Never commit tokens, keys, credentials, or `.env` files.
- Use no paid API keys.
- Avoid runtime secrets entirely.

## Dependencies

- Prefer mature, maintained open-source packages.
- Minimise dependency count.
- Run an audit and review high-severity issues.
- Do not automatically apply destructive dependency upgrades without testing.

## Images

Use only:

- Public-domain images
- Clearly licensed Wikimedia Commons assets
- Official press images with explicit reuse permission
- Original illustrations
- Institution-provided reusable media

Store:

- Creator
- Source URL
- Licence
- Licence URL
- Attribution

Do not hotlink unstable or unauthorised images.

## Copyright

- Paraphrase biographies.
- Do not reproduce full articles.
- Do not reproduce long award citations beyond reasonable quotation.
- Link to original papers rather than copying them.
- Keep quotations brief and sourced.

## Privacy

- No user accounts are required.
- No behavioural tracking is required.
- Avoid cookies unless essential.
- Prefer no analytics or privacy-friendly self-hosted analytics.
- Provide a privacy page describing actual behaviour.

## Ethical Representation

- Avoid ranking mathematicians as objectively best.
- Avoid unsupported claims about intelligence or identity.
- Treat nationality and affiliation carefully.
- Explain incomplete and disputed data.
- Do not imply award success is a guaranteed career outcome.
